Content Suggestions

If you know of an architecturally significant museum that should be included in our directory, we'd love to hear about it. Please provide:

  • Museum name (in English and Japanese if available)
  • Location (city and prefecture)
  • Architect and construction/completion date
  • Architectural significance (style, innovation, cultural importance)
  • Source for verification (official website, architectural records, etc.)

Technical Issues

Experiencing problems with the website? Let us know by including:

  • Description of the issue you encountered
  • Your browser and device type
  • Which page or feature was affected
  • Screenshots if applicable

Corrections and Updates

Found outdated or incorrect information? Help us maintain accuracy by providing:

  • Specific museum listing that needs correction
  • Description of what needs updating
  • Source for updated information (official museum website, recent visit, etc.)
  • Date of information if relevant

Partnership & Media Inquiries

For partnership opportunities, media inquiries, or collaboration proposals, please email us at wfhfujisan@outlook.jp with:

  • Your organization or publication name
  • Nature of the inquiry or proposal
  • Relevant background information
  • Preferred timeline (if applicable)

Response Time

⏱️ What to Expect

Standard Inquiries: We aim to respond to all emails within 3-5 business days.

Peak Travel Seasons: During cherry blossom season (March-April) and autumn foliage season (October-November), response times may extend to 5-7 business days due to higher inquiry volume.

Urgent Matters: For time-sensitive travel questions, please indicate "URGENT" in your email subject line, and we'll prioritize your inquiry.

Office Hours

Japan Standard Time (JST):

  • Monday - Friday: 9:00 AM - 6:00 PM JST
  • Weekends & Holidays: Inquiries received on weekends will be addressed on the next business day

Note: Japan Standard Time is UTC+9. Use a time zone converter if you're contacting us from outside Japan.

Location

This website is maintained by an independent cultural researcher based in Japan with professional experience in Japanese cultural institutions and international cultural exchange programs, specializing in museums, galleries, and architectural heritage.

Privacy & Data Protection

Any information you provide through email correspondence will be used solely to respond to your inquiry and will not be shared with third parties. We respect your privacy and handle all communications confidentially.

For more information about how we handle data, please see our Privacy Policy.

Language Note

We accept inquiries in both English and Japanese. While our website content is primarily in English to serve international visitors, we're happy to communicate in Japanese for more complex questions about specific museums or regional travel planning.
